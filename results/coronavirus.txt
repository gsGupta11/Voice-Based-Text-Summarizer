Coronaviruses are important pathogens in house cats;  feline infectious peritonitis virus (FIPV) and feline enteric coronavirus (FECV) are essentially the same virus and can cause fairly serious disease in places like cat breeding facilities where cats are kept in close quarters. Members of Coronavirus cause: hepatitis (inflammation of the liver) in mice gastroenteritis (inflammation of the digestive system) in pigs, canines and feline species respiratory infections (basically, the common cold) in birds and human beings There is a vaccine for feline coronavirus, but there is no good treatment for a cat once it is sick, though some vets have used corticosteroid therapy with varying success.
